### III. The ParseKernel as Ground of Being

Ch01 defines the ParseKernel: the irreducible grammar that distinguishes

> *What was a constraint in 4D becomes a generator in 5D.*

The ParseKernel in E01 is the grammar of being itself. It does not ask

emergence that E01 enacts.

lawful. The ParseKernel-as-generator is an inexhaustible source: given

because every valid structure is, by definition, an output of the
kernel's generative grammar.

### V. The TruthCorridor as Unique Path

The uniqueness clause -- "every coordinate admits exactly one truth
corridor" -- becomes, in E01, the statement that the topology is

guarantee that the seed does not collapse into undifferentiated mush.

truth corridor remains its own -- even as all three are compressed
into a single emergent cell.
