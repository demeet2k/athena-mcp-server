All codified Voynich languages are conjugate views of one executable manifold:

`operator -> carrier -> transition -> checkpoint -> compression -> lift`

- is the change lawful,
- is the carrier stable enough,
- is the boundary respected,
- is the repetition sufficient,
- is the checkpoint real,

- Chemistry catches material confusion.
