## Page Grammar and Structural Signature

The local evidence supports a four-block sequence:

1. `P1`: apparatus chain and base setup
2. `P2`: volatile capture under fire-sealed control
3. `P3`: full main-cycle procedure with repeated checks
4. `P4`: collection, verification, and terminal fixing

The strongest load-bearing pattern is the unusually dense apparatus vocabulary:

- `CTH` / conduit-throat
- `CKH` / clamp-valve
- `CPH` / pressure vessel or still-head

This makes `f1r` function less like a recipe and more like a lab-opening protocol.
