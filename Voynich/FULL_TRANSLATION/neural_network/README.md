## Why This Layer Exists

The folio corpus tells us what each page means. The section and crystal layers tell us what each book means. The neural layer asks a different question: what happens when every document-class in the whole project is allowed to interact with every other document-class under a consistent registry, route logic, and reseeding law?

The second pass adds the information layer because the first neural pass still kept information implicit inside the elemental field. This pass names which nodes are high-entropy payload zones, which are compression surfaces, which are replay and runtime governance surfaces, and which handoffs are lawful versus lossy.

The new 4D organism layer adds a further distinction: the corpus is not only a neural field of interacting documents, but a mycelial nervous organism whose minimal mature unit is the proof-carrying packet moving through address, corridor, burden, and replay space.

The expansion bundle pushes that one step further by explicitly tying the Voynich organ into neighboring Athena tissues: `mycelium_brain`, `nervous_system`, the deeper integrated network, the precursor nervous-system mirror, the cortex overview shell, and the `MATH` operator field.

Pass 3 pushes it further again by naming the `64` sleeve surface, the vertical brain stem, the fascial connective tissues, and the denser cross-corpus synaptic matrix.

Pass 4 pushes deeper again by decomposing the body into `256` microcells through `16 nodes x 4 dimensions x 4 operations`, integrating the control plane directly into stem/fascia law, and promoting the organism from macro-connected to fine-grained self-regulating.

Pass 5 pushes deeper again by decomposing those `256` microcells through the four control-plane surfaces, yielding `1024` neurocontrol fibers and promoting the organism from self-regulating tissue into governance-bearing nervous matter.

Pass 6 pushes deeper again by projecting those `1024` neurocontrol fibers through the four manuscript lenses, yielding `4096` weighted nexus points and promoting the organism from governance-bearing tissue into a true stored coordinate field of physical crossings.

## Zero Point

The neural layer treats the corpus as a self-routing manuscript machine. The information layer clarifies why: every document becomes more valuable when it is read not only for its local claim, but for the kind of information it stores, the entropy it introduces, the compression it requires, the handoff it enables, and the replay burden it passes forward.
