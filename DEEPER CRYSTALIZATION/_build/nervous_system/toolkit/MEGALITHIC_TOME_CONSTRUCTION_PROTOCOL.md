# MEGALITHIC TOME CONSTRUCTION PROTOCOL

## Mission

Construct large-scale tomes with explicit addressing, mycelium edges, router rules, and chapter-appendix crystal integrity.

## Mandatory Layers

1. abstract contract
2. extended abstract
3. station-level metro map
4. appendix crystal
5. deterministic router
6. chapter skeleton
7. appendix tiles

## Construction Law

Each chapter must be a full 4^4 tile and each appendix must act as both content and routing hub.

## Stop Law

If the requested output is Part 1 only, do not continue into Parts 2 or 3.
