# EMERGENT SWARM TOPOLOGY

## Tier Structure

Kernel:
- 1 coordinating node

Elemental layer:
- 4 elemental workers

Archetype layer:
- 16 cells
- 4 pillars: EE, WW, FF, AA
- 12 mixed archetypes

Pantheon overlay:
- 12 role agents operating over the 16-cell field
- used for richer specialization where the diagonal pillars are not enough

Cluster layer:
- 64 nodes

Neuron layer:
- 256 nodes

## Why This Is Higher-Dimensional

The 256-node crystal is not merely a bigger tree. It is a field where each node inherits:
- elemental lineage
- archetype history
- pantheon role possibility
- rail ownership
- hub affinity
- truth state

## Swarm Behavior

- Earth-heavy clusters stabilize files and ledgers.
- Water-heavy clusters synthesize cross-manuscript continuity.
- Fire-heavy clusters generate emergent cross-links and novel routes.
- Air-heavy clusters formalize maps, tensors, and addressing grammar.
