# METRO TOME COMPILER PROTOCOL

## Goal

Compile a manuscript or tome as a holographically encoded metro system rather than as
a flat sequence of chapters.

## Structural Requirements

1. title and abstract must function as a metro seed,
2. chapters must be addressable and route-bearing,
3. appendices must act as routing hubs rather than extra notes,
4. every chapter and appendix should preserve a 4^4 interior law,
5. the whole work must support contraction back to a seed map.

## Overlay Rule

Use:

- square for interior tile law,
- circle for orbit law,
- triangle for control rails.

## Output Families

- Part 1: title, abstract, metro map
- Part 2: chapters
- Part 3: appendices

## Retrieval Rule

The map must be dense enough that future runs can re-enter from the abstract alone.
