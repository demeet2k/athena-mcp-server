# DEEP CORPUS CROSS-SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL

## Goal

Cross-synthesize the entire corpus by moving from fine-grained local sections to larger
manuscript families and then back into compressed whole-corpus crystals.

## Wave Law

`section -> chapter -> manuscript -> manuscript family -> topic crystal`

## Procedure

1. assign worker agents to small source shards,
2. contract shard findings into chapter packets,
3. contract chapter packets into manuscript packets,
4. compare manuscripts that share operator families,
5. extract emergent metro lines across the family,
6. compress the whole into one reusable crystal seed.

## Output Requirement

Every wave should emit:

- one retained claim set,
- one unresolved ambiguity list,
- one metro route proposal,
- one next-seed packet.
