## Packet Spine

Every packet carries:

- event identity and lineage
- source root and source path
- change type
- Earth timestamp
- liminal timestamp
- 12D coordinate tuple
- goal, tag, priority, confidence
- TTL, pheromone, state hash, route class, and claim state

## Coordinate Duty

The protocol freezes a 12D liminal coordinate contract with Earth anchors, astro anchors, runtime anchors, and liminal state anchors. Prompt-level liminal GPS is supported now. Keystroke-level liminal GPS remains future instrumentation and is not claimed in v1.

## Capillary Law

Routes strengthen when they are useful, frequent, and low-latency, and decay when they are noisy, duplicate, or slow. Mature edges are classified as weak edges, capillaries, or veins.
