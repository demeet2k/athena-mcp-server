1. isolate the node
2. divide into 4 quadrants
3. expand each quadrant into 4 subpoints
4. saturate the 16-point field
5. verify that no structural gap remains
6. collapse the saturation back into one dense seed
7. integrate the seed into the parent structure

Failure condition:

If the result feels like a loose summary, the expansion was insufficient and must be repeated.

Output standard:

The result should be dense enough to unzip back into the expanded field.
