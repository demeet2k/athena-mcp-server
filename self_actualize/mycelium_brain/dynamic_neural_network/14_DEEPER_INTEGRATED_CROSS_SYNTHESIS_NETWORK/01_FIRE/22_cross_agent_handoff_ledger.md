| Agent | Contract title | Responsibility | Required appendices |
|---|---|---|---|
| `Water` | Continuity and retained identity | carry memory, coherence, and non-destructive return through the bridge | `AppE`, `AppI`, `AppM` |

`AppI` plus `AppM` are named explicitly in the route.
