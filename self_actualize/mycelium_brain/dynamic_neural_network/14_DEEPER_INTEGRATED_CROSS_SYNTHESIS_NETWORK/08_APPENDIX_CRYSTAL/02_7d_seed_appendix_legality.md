## Legality rules

- `Q` docks only through canonical `AppQ`.
- reverse-field `O` remains an overlay alias and may not replace canonical `AppO`.
- every reverse station in `Z->K` must publish one or more canonical `App*` mappings.
- `AppK` is required whenever contradiction survives.
- `AppN` is required whenever `H6`, `H7`, `Seed-6D`, or `Seed-7D` persists as a carrier state.
- `AppP` is required whenever a route claims stable or deployable return.

## Promotion floor

No route promotes into `7D_SEED` without explicit `AppI` truth basis and `AppM` replay
basis.
