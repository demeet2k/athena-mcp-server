# 4D Plus Economy Law

The deeper network is a replay surface inside the wider organism and therefore follows:

`Theta_plus = (AddressMesh, RelationMetro, ChamberMembrane, ReplayReturnAxis; EconomySalienceBudget)`

- `AddressMesh`: where a basis document or appendix support surface lives.
- `RelationMetro`: which corridor, metro line, or ordered pair carries the bridge.
- `ChamberMembrane`: which truth, burden, or appendix shell constrains the move.
- `ReplayReturnAxis`: how the move rebuilds, compresses, and reseeds.
- `EconomySalienceBudget`: which valid move receives scarce build attention first.
