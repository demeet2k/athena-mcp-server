## Split Rule

When many agents are active:

1. assign by family first;
2. then by manuscript;
3. then by chapter;
4. then by section only if needed.

## Contraction Rule

When each family front lands a reusable artifact, contract upward in reverse:

1. section to chapter
2. chapter to manuscript
3. manuscript to family
4. family to corpus
