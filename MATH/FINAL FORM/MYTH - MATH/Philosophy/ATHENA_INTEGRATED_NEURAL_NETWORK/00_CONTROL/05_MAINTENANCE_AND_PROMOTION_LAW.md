# Maintenance and Promotion Law

Source basis: `00_CORE/14_change_ledger.md + 00_CORE/15_promotion_contract.md + LEDGERS/`
Interpretation rule: Maintenance is local, explicit, and cumulative; promotion is dry-run by default and only becomes real after an explicit later request clears the named gates.

This control file is package-specific. It is part of the hybrid-mirror control plane for `ATHENA_INTEGRATED_NEURAL_NETWORK`, not a copied law sheet from the older live deeper-network root.

## Control Law

Nothing leaves the package merely because it is complete. Governance, ledgers, micro-skills, and drift surfaces remain local unless promotion is explicitly requested and support remains intact.

## Operational Consequences

- Change tracking is a first-class artifact, not an afterthought.
- Promotion readiness complements the promotion contract but does not execute it.
- Package-only operational surfaces are protected by default.

## Zero-point Compression

At zero point, maintenance and promotion collapse to one law: preserve honest local truth before any outward motion.
