# Cohesion And Pruning

This pass does not prune by deletion. It prunes by classification, precedence, and de-prioritization.

The precedence order is:

1. live deep-root authority
2. historical witness layers
3. family-local only orchestrators

Live authority means the Earth loop files under the current `14_DEEPER...` root plus the current appendix crystal and skill router. Historical witness means older Earth passes and mirror roots remain readable for comparison but cannot overrule the live deep root. Family-local only means a surface may still be useful inside its own subsystem, but it cannot claim to be the whole-corpus router.

This is the structural answer to bloat: keep what still witnesses, but stop letting every old surface pretend to be current authority.
