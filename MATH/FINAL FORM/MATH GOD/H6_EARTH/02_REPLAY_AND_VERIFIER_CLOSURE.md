# Replay And Verifier Closure

Earth proves value by making routes replayable rather than merely persuasive.

In this layer, replay closure means:

- every sample route receipt must point back to an existing `route_plan_id`,
- the route receipt must preserve the source truth state,
- the replay pointer must remain intact,
- the legality context must remain attached through the appendix support stack.

Verifier closure means the Earth layer does not promote ambiguous or failed material by rhetoric. It keeps corridor truth visible:

- `OK` stays witnessed and replay-safe,
- `NEAR` stays bounded,
- `AMBIG` stays explicit and evidence-hungry,
- `FAIL` stays quarantined.

This is why the Earth layer depends structurally on `AppA`, `AppI`, and `AppM`. Addressing, corridor truth, and replay kernel cannot be separated without breaking the bundle.
