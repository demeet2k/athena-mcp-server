## Interpretation Law

- `physical` measures disk body, not searchable recall
- `indexed` measures searchable live atlas presence, not the full disk
- `board` measures runtime-visible swarm scope, not the canonical whole
- `archive` measures hidden indexed reserve, not already-live promotion
- `promoted` measures metabolized nervous-system slice, not total corpus breadth
- `generated` measures indexed shell mass inside the live atlas, not a parallel witness class

## Citation Law

Every summary surface must name the witness class when it cites a count.

Approved examples:

- `Indexed witness: 7713`
- `Board witness: 9978`
- `Generated indexed shell: 3535`

Unapproved examples:

- `Athena has 7559 files`
- `The organism is 9978`
- `The project is 1818`
