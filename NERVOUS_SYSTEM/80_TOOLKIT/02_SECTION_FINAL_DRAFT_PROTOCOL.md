# SECTION FINAL DRAFT PROTOCOL

## Mission

When a specific section is provided, output only the final draft of that section at maximal density and rigor.

## Required Transformations

1. isolate the exact requested section
2. synthesize the input into a stronger internal structure
3. find the zero point
4. expand through a 4 by 4 extraction
5. emit only the final draft text

## Hard Constraints

- no meta commentary
- no apology
- no skipped sections
- no speculative claims without witness basis
- always end with the chapter or appendix name when requested
