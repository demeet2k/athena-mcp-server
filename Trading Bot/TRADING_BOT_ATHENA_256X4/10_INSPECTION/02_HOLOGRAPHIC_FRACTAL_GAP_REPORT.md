# Holographic Fractal Gap Report

## Gap 1: Breadth exceeds density
The framework expanded quickly into many files, but its chapter and appendix prose remained too thin.

## Gap 2: Metro is named more than traversed
The lines and hubs exist, but route proofs are still sparse.

## Gap 3: Swarm is named more than behaviorally integrated
Roles, task cells, and atoms exist, but ownership is not yet propagated into enough concrete examples.

## Gap 4: The fractal loop is stated more than executed
Infinite recursion is present as doctrine, but the self-improvement loop was not yet a first-class corpus surface.

## Gap 5: Cross-plane integration is shallow
Many plane cells point to one another, but there was no dedicated plan body explaining how to strengthen those couplings over time.
